Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural response to stress and can be beneficial in certain situations. However, when anxiety becomes overwhelming, it can interfere with daily activities.

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or fear about something that may happen. It can be a normal reaction to stressful situations, but excessive anxiety may lead to disorders.

Common Symptoms

  • Restlessness
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulties concentrating
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Physical symptoms such as increased heart rate

Managing Anxiety

There are several effective strategies to manage anxiety, including:

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Regular physical activity
  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Talking to a therapist or counselor
